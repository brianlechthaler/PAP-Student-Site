Frequently Asked Questions

If you need help one way or another come here.

1. Can I change my schedule?
Yes! Go talk to Lisa about your schedule change. Go to the Office or email Lisa at lisa@paloaltoprep.com.
2. Who are the teachers?
If you need to know who the teachers are just go to this page.
3. What's my schedule or what are my grades?
At Palo Alto Prep we have a website called Aeries. Through this, we can email teachers, check our grades, and look at out schedule. If you need to set up and Aeries account go to Matt at the front desk.
4. Why is my teacher not grading my papers?
Teachers are busy. If you have missing assignments, teachers have until the next wednsday to grade it. If it is still missing, POLITELY ask you teacher about it.
5. Why am I in/What is Friday School?
If you have a C- or lower in any class than you are put on the Friday School list. Talk to the teacher of the class where you have a bad grade. If you skip Friday School than you have to do 5 hours of Community Service.
6. I have a suggustion for the site
Great! Please talk to Aidan or anyone else on the credits page. You could also go to the Computer Club meeting on Thursdays in the Aviation Room. If you are a tech person you can fork us on github.

Ask a teacher!
